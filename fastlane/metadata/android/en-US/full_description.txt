Screen Wakelock Detector logs when your phone screen turns on and shows which app or notification channel most likely caused it.

<b>What it does</b>
• Detects every screen-on event in the background
• Identifies the likely app and notification channel
• Shows a searchable history with confidence scores
• Opens notification settings or mutes a channel in one tap

<b>Privacy</b>
Everything stays on your device. No account, no cloud upload, no analytics. Notification listener access reads metadata (app, channel, time)—not message text.

<b>Optional root</b>
On rooted devices, enable in-app root diagnostics for deeper wakelock attribution. All tooling is built into the app—no Shizuku, Magisk modules, or companion apps.

<b>Permissions explained</b>
The app asks for each permission only when needed, with clear skip paths. Basic screen-on logging works even if you decline optional grants.
